Lesson 6 — Present Continuous: Happening Now

We use the present continuous to talk about an action that is happening now, at this moment. The structure is am / is / are + verb-ing.

Key language

Structure: subject + am / is / are + verb-ing

  • I am working. / He is working. / They are working.
  • Em português: "está trabalhando / a trabalhar". O inglês precisa do verbo "to be" (am/is/are) — não pode esquecer!

Which form of "be"?

  • I → am
  • he / she / it → is
  • you / we / they → are

-ing spelling rules

  • Most verbs: just add -ing → work → working, eat → eating, read → reading
  • Verbs ending in -e: drop the e → write → writing, make → making
  • Short verb, consonant + vowel + consonant: double the last consonant → run → running, sit → sitting
  • Em português, o gerúndio é regular ("-ndo"). Em inglês há pequenas regras de escrita — atenção ao "e" e à consoante dupla.

Time markers (signs of "now")

  • now, right now, at the moment, today

Now vs every day

  • Now (present continuous): I am working now.
  • Every day (present simple): I work every day.
  • "Now" → está acontecendo agora. "Every day" → rotina.

Grammar trap: English always needs "to be" + -ing. Write "She is working", NOT "She working".
