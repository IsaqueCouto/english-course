Lesson 3 — Questions: Yes/No and Wh-

You already know how to write present-simple sentences. Today you learn how to turn them into questions and how to write short answers. Read the rules, then write your answers in the spaces.

Key language

Yes/no questions use a helper word at the front: do or does.

  • Do + I / you / we / they + base verb? → Do you work here?
  • Does + he / she / it + base verb? → Does she work here?
  • PT: O português não tem o auxiliar do/does. Em inglês a pergunta precisa de do/does no começo.

The base-verb rule (important). After do/does, the main verb has no -s.

  • Does he work? ✓ Does he works? ✗
  • PT: Depois de do/does, o verbo fica na forma base (sem -s). O does já carrega o -s.

Wh- questions start with a question word: what, where, when, who, how, why. Word order: question word + do/does + subject + base verb?

  • Where do you live? What does she like? When does the class start?
  • PT: A palavra de pergunta vem primeiro, depois do/does, depois o sujeito, depois o verbo base.

Short answers reuse do/does (not the main verb).

  • Do you work here? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
  • Does she live here? — Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.

Practice

(a) Turn each statement into a yes/no question

PT: Transforme a frase em pergunta de sim/não com do ou does.

Example: You like coffee. → Do you like coffee?

  1. They live in Mexico. → _______________________________
  2. He works on Saturday. → _______________________________
  3. You speak English. → _______________________________
  4. She likes her job. → _______________________________

(b) Complete each Wh- question with the right question word

Word bank: what · where · when · who · how · why PT: Complete a pergunta com a palavra de pergunta correta.

Example: Where does she live? — In a small city.

  1. _______ do you start work? — At nine o'clock.
  2. _______ does he study English? — Because he wants a new job.
  3. _______ do they eat for lunch? — A sandwich.
  4. _______ is your new manager? — Her name is Ana.

(c) Write the short answer (Yes, … / No, …)

PT: Escreva a resposta curta usando do/does.

Example: Do you work here? (yes) → Yes, I do.

  1. Does she live near the office? (no) → _______________________
  2. Do they speak English? (yes) → _______________________
  3. Does he like coffee? (yes) → _______________________
  4. Do you work on Sunday? (no) → _______________________

(d) Error correction

Each question has one mistake. Cross it out and write the correct question. PT: Cada pergunta tem um erro. Corrija.

Example: Do she works here? → Does she work here?

  1. Does he works on Monday? → _______________________________
  2. Where you live? → _______________________________
  3. Does they like the city? → _______________________________
  4. What she does for work? → _______________________________

(e) Read and write questions

Read the short paragraph. Then write 3–4 questions you could ask about this person. PT: Leia o texto e escreva 3-4 perguntas sobre a pessoa.

Carlos is from Mexico. He lives in a big city now. He works in a hotel. He starts work at seven o'clock. He likes his job, and he studies English in the evening.

Example: Where does Carlos live?

  4. _______________________________________________________ (optional)

(f) Write questions for a new coworker

Use the sentence starters to write 4–5 questions you would ask a new coworker. PT: Use os inícios de frase para escrever 4-5 perguntas para um colega de trabalho novo.

Example starter: Where do you … → Where do you live?

  1. Where do you _______________________________________?
  2. What time do you _______________________________________?
  3. Do you _______________________________________?
  4. How do you _______________________________________?
  5. Why do you _______________________________________? (optional)

Homework

  1. Rewrite these four statements as yes/no questions:
    • You work in an office. → __________________________
    • She lives near here. → __________________________
    • They like the city. → __________________________
    • He studies English. → __________________________
  2. Complete these four Wh- questions with the right question word (what / where / when / who / how / why):
    • _______ do you go after work?
    • _______ time does the meeting start?
    • _______ do you study English?
    • _______ is your teacher?
  3. Write a short answer for each:
    • Do you work on Friday? (yes) → __________________________
    • Does she like coffee? (no) → __________________________
    • Do they live in a city? (yes) → __________________________

Remember: after do/does, the verb has no -s.
