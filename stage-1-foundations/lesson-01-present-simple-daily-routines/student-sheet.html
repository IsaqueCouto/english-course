Lesson 1 — Present Simple: Daily Routines

Welcome to Stage 1! You already write simple sentences well. Now you will learn to write about your daily routine — what you do every day. In this lesson you will use only I, you, we, they.

Key language

Routine verbs

  • wake up — acordar
  • get up — levantar-se
  • have breakfast — tomar o café da manhã
  • have lunch — almoçar
  • have dinner — jantar
  • go to work — ir para o trabalho
  • start work — começar a trabalhar
  • finish work — terminar o trabalho
  • come home — chegar em casa
  • study — estudar
  • watch TV — assistir TV
  • go to bed — ir para a cama

Grammar rules

1. Subject + verb + time. With I/you/we/they the verb stays plain (no -s).

I work in the morning. — Com I/you/we/they o verbo não muda.

2. Negative = don't + verb.

I don't work at night. — don't = não (forma negativa).

3. Frequency adverbs go BEFORE the verb: always, usually, sometimes, never.

I always get up early. / I never study at night. — Esses advérbios vêm antes do verbo.

Time expressions

  • at 7:00 (at + hora)
  • in the morning / in the afternoon / in the evening (in + parte do dia)
  • at night (à noite)
  • every day (todos os dias)

Practice

(a) Write full sentences from the prompts

Use a verb + a time expression. Escreva frases completas.

Word bank (time expressions): at 7:00 · in the morning · in the afternoon · in the evening · at night · every day

Example: (I / wake up) → I wake up at 7:00 in the morning.

  1. (I / have breakfast) → _______________________________________________
  2. (We / go to work) → _______________________________________________
  3. (They / have lunch) → _______________________________________________
  4. (You / go to bed) → _______________________________________________

(b) Make these sentences negative with don't

Torne as frases negativas usando don't.

Example: I work on Sunday. → I don't work on Sunday.

  1. We study in the evening. → _______________________________________________
  2. They watch TV every day. → _______________________________________________
  3. I have dinner at 6:00. → _______________________________________________

(c) Gap-fill — choose the right word

Complete com a palavra correta do banco.

Word bank: at · in · don't · always · finish

Example: I get up at 7:00.

  1. We have lunch _______ the afternoon.
  2. I _______ study at night. (negative)
  3. They _______ start work in the morning. (frequency adverb = every time)
  4. You _______ work at 5:00 in the evening.

(d) Error correction

Each sentence has ONE clear mistake. Write the correct sentence. Corrija o erro.

Example: I works in the morning. → I work in the morning.

  1. We no study on Saturday. → _______________________________________________
  2. I get up always early. → _______________________________________________
  3. They have lunch in 1:00. → _______________________________________________

(e) Read and answer

Read the paragraph, then answer the questions in writing. Leia e responda por escrito.

Anna and Pedro are from Mexico. They wake up at 6:30 every day. Pedro starts work in the morning, but Anna works in the evening. They always have dinner together at night. On Sunday they don't go to work — they watch TV and study English.

  1. What time do Anna and Pedro wake up? _______________________________________
  2. When does Anna work? _______________________________________________
  3. What do they always do at night? _______________________________________
  4. Do they go to work on Sunday? _______________________________________

(f) Write your own routine

Write 4–5 sentences about YOUR typical day. Use the sentence starters below. Escreva 4–5 frases sobre o seu dia.

Example: I wake up at 6:00 in the morning.

  1. I wake up _______________________________________________
  2. In the morning, I _______________________________________________
  3. I usually _______________________________________________
  4. In the evening, I _______________________________________________
  5. I don't _______________________________________________

Homework

  1. Finish any Practice items you did not complete in class.
  2. Write a short paragraph (4–5 sentences) about a family member's typical day. Use "they": for example, They get up at 8:00. They go to work in the morning.
  3. Write 3 negative sentences with "don't" about things you do NOT do on weekends. Example: I don't start work on Saturday.
